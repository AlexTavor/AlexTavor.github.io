What I build

Backends and systems where the hard part is getting it right: data pipelines, orchestration and tooling, agentic / LLM systems, and the correctness-critical core that everything else hangs off. Greenfield where the contract can be made stable, or untangling something that already exists and has become hard to change.

How I work

Spec-first and gated — a method I call Proof-Driven Development. Pin down what "correct" means before writing code, make the checks actually bite, and keep the work reviewable in small pieces. It is slower to start and far cheaper to live with. The chdr write-up is where it came from and shows it in practice.

You get straight answers about tradeoffs — including when not to build something, or when to stop. I would rather lose the work than ship you something I know is the wrong call.

Engagements
